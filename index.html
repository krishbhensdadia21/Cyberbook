Welcome to the World of Cyber Security

🛡️ Why Cyber Security Matters


📌 It helps protect your data from theft.

📌 Prevents unauthorized access.

📌 Shields you from viruses, malware, and ransomware.

📌 Ensures privacy and digital trust.

🚨 Common Cyber Threats


💣 Malicious software like viruses or trojans.

🎣 Fake emails to trick users into revealing personal info.

🧑‍💻 Unauthorized access to computer systems.

🦠 Locks your files until you pay a ransom.


🔑 Tips to Stay Safe


💾 Keep software updated regularly.

📧 Don’t click suspicious links or attachments.

🧩 Use strong, unique passwords.

🔐 Enable 2-factor authentication.

🕵️‍♂️ Use antivirus and firewalls.
